About Us

Welcome to Sattva Mother's Homely Food, where every meal is a labor of love. Cooking has always been my passion, and the kitchen is where my heart truly feels at home. I've always cherished the joy of preparing meals for my family and loved ones, and that warmth now extends to every dish we serve. At Sattva, we believe that the secret ingredient to truly delicious food is love — combined with carefully handpicked ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations. Our motto, "Delicious pure traditional meals just how amma makes," reflects our commitment to bringing you the comfort, purity, and authenticity of home-cooked food in every bite.
